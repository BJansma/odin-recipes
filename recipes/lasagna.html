Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Mozzarella slices
  4. Meat sauce
  5. Parmesan cheese
  6. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan.
